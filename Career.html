Sporting Lisbon (2002-2003):

Sporting Lisbon (2002-2003): Ronaldo began his professional career with Sporting Lisbon in Portugal. His performances caught the attention of Manchester United scouts during a pre-season friendly, leading to his transfer. Manchester United (2003-2009): Ronaldo joined Manchester United in 2003 under manager Sir Alex Ferguson. During his time at the club, he became one of the world's best players. He won three English Premier League titles, one FA Cup, two Football League Cups, and the UEFA Champions League in the 2007-2008 season. Ronaldo's individual awards included the FIFA World Player of the Year in 2008. Real Madrid (2009-2018): In 2009, Ronaldo made a then-world-record transfer to Real Madrid. His time with Real Madrid was incredibly successful, marked by four Champions League titles, two La Liga titles, and numerous individual awards. Ronaldo became Real Madrid's all-time leading goal-scorer, breaking several records along the way. Juventus (2018-2021): Ronaldo transferred to Juventus in 2018, making an impact in Italian Serie A. He helped Juventus secure several domestic trophies, including Serie A titles. Return to Manchester United (2021-present): In August 2021, Ronaldo made a highly anticipated return to Manchester United. His return generated immense excitement among fans.
